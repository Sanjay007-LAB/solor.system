Solar System
The Sun,the eight planets with their sattilites,and some other heavenly bodies like asteroids,meteors and comets form the Solar System.The Sun is at the centre of the Solar System

The Sun

The Sun is the largest member in the Solar System.All the planets,satellites,asteroids and comets revolve around it. It is about 150 million km away from the Earth.It produced lot of heat and light which is necessary for all forms of life on Earth

The Planets
